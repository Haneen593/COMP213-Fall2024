Website about Proteced Areas Issues

Protected areas play a key role for the conservation biodiversity, ecosystems, and endangered species. In today's rapidly changing world, they provide refuges for wildlife and safeguard natural resources, which are essential for human well-being. Establishing and maintaining conservation areas helps combat climate change, protect water resources, and ensure that future generations inherit a world rich in biodiversity.
